Who We Are?

The Lady Shop produces high-quality women's clothing. It features a classic elegant chic look. The suits are comfortable, with precise cuts and a slim silhouette. In company stores, ladies will find various models of jackets, skirts, pants, dresses, blouses, coats, with ready suggestions for combinations. ANDREWS/ladies is committed to ensuring that clothing meets the highest quality standards. In pursuit of elegance, the company's design team follows fashion trends by creating a seasonal variety of luxury clothing.

Lady shop offers its demanding customers two main collections per year, complemented by intermediate "last minute" series that capture the hottest novelties.
